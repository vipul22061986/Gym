Childs Pose
0
Home
Target Body Part: Abs, Back, Butt/Hips, Legs – Thighs
Equipment Needed: No Equipment

  • Come to a hands and knees position on the mat with your toes pointing behind you, big toes touching
  • Widen your knees towards the outside of the mat as you sit back toward your heels.
  • As you sit back, try to bring your hips all the way to your heels or as far back as possible. Stretch out through your back and arms.
  • Pull the shoulders down and back. Keep them away from your ears. Allow your chest to rest between your thighs and your forehead to rest on the floor.
  • Inhale as you lengthen the spine, extending through the upper body, and exhale as you fold deeper into the pose, widening the knees as needed.
  • Hold this position for 5-10 slow, deep breaths (approximately 30 seconds to a minute).
  • Exercise variation: Rest your arms alongside the body (toward your hips) with the palms facing upward and the fronts of your shoulders toward the floor.

Abs Workout

*Disclaimer

Consult a physician before beginning any exercise program.

How to Use this Program

For someone new to exercise, you can do this program at home without any equipment. Simply follow the recommended workout and you'll begin to see results and feel better in only a few weeks. This program will help flatten your tummy while concurrently developing strength and definition in the hips and thighs.

For people who are already exercising on a regular basis, this program can be used as a supplement when you don't have time to make it to the gym and need a quick routine you can do from home or on the road while traveling. Another option is to use this program as a dynamic warm-up to prepare the core for the challenges of your regular exercise routine.

Frequency:

This program could be done two-to-three times per week, with at least one full day of rest between workouts.

Intensity:

When starting the program complete each exercise for 1-3 sets resting between each set before moving to the next exercise. To burn more calories; turn the routine into a circuit and complete one exercise right after the other and rest for 2-3 minutes after the completion of one circuit (all exercises).

Workout #1

Hip Bridge

Target Body Part: Abs, Butt/Hips

Equipment Needed: No Equipment

  • Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor, arms by your sides.
  • Lift your hips off the floor until your back forms a straight line from knees to shoulders.
  • Hold this peak position for a 2 second contraction, really squeezing your hamstrings and glutes.
  • lower your hips back to the starting position and repeat.

Childs Pose

Target Body Part: Abs, Back, Butt/Hips, Legs – Thighs

Equipment Needed: No Equipment

  • Come to a hands and knees position on the mat with your toes pointing behind you, big toes touching.
  • Widen your knees towards the outside of the mat as you sit back toward your heels.
  • As you sit back, try to bring your hips all the way to your heels or as far back as possible. Stretch out through your back and arms.
  • Pull the shoulders down and back. Keep them away from your ears. Allow your chest to rest between your thighs and your forehead to rest on the floor.
  • Inhale as you lengthen the spine, extending through the upper body, and exhale as you fold deeper into the pose, widening the knees as needed.
  • Hold this position for 5-10 slow, deep breaths (approximately 30 seconds to a minute).
  • Exercise variation: Rest your arms alongside the body (toward your hips) with the palms facing upward and the fronts of your shoulders toward the floor.

Sit-Ups

Target Body Part: Abs

Equipment Needed: No Equipment

  • Lie flat on your back on the floor with your knees bent at 90 degrees and feet flat on the floor. Place your hands behind your head and lock them together by clasping your fingers.
  • Raise your upper body so that it creates an imaginary V-shape with your thighs.
  • Once you feel the contraction for a second, lower your upper body back down to the starting position.

Side Bends

Target Body Part: Abs

Equipment Needed: No Equipment

  • Begin with feet slightly wider than shoulder-width apart
  • Slowly bend to the right as far as you can
  • Pause, then slowly return to an upright position.
  • Repeat, bending to the left side.

Reverse Marches

Target Body Part: Abs

Equipment Needed: No Equipment

  • Lie on your back on a mat in a bent-knee position with feet placed firmly on the floor, arms outstretched to your sides in a "T" position; palms facing upward.
  • Slowly lift one foot off the floor while keeping your knee bent at 90-degrees.
  • Hold this position for 3-5 seconds
  • Return to the starting position and repeat with your alternate leg

  • Then using your abdominal muscles kick your right leg out in front of you.

  • Repeat for desired reps.

Bicycle Crunches

Target Body Part: Abs

Equipment Needed: No Equipment

  • Lie flat on the floor with your lower back pressed to the ground and contract your core muscles.
  • With your hands gently holding your head, lift your knees to about a 45-degree angle.
  • Slowly, go through a bicycle pedal motion kicking forward with the right leg and bringing in the knee of the left leg.
  • Alternately touching your elbows to the opposite knees as you twist back and forth.
  • Continue alternating until all recommended amount of repetitions have been completed.

Side Scissor Kicks

Target Body Part: Abs

Equipment Needed: No Equipment

Starting Position: Lie on your back with your legs extended. Place your hands flat on the ground with palms down, wedging them under your butt slightly.

  • Raise your feet about a foot off the ground.
  • Kick your feet back and forth from side-to-side..

High-Low Plank

Target Body Part: Abs, Arms, Chest, Shoulders

Equipment Needed: No Equipment Needed

  • Begin in a high plank position on your hands, lower down to your elbows one at a time, then raise back up to your hands.
  • Start out on the right side first, then on the left side.
  • Repeat for desired reps.

Bow Extension

Target Body Part: Abs

Equipment Needed: Dumbbell (Or pick up any object you can grip easily, like a phone book, bottle of water or a closed laptop computer.)

  • Hold a dumbbell in both hands above your right shoulder and point your left foot out to the side.
  • In one motion, draw your elbows down to lower the weight as you bend your left knee toward your chest.
  • Reverse the movement to return to start. That's one rep.
  • Perform the suggested number of reps, then switch sides and repeat.

Reverse Plank

Target Body Part: Abs, Back, Triceps, Shoulders

Equipment Needed: No Equipment

  • Sit down with legs positioned pointing directly out in front of you.
  • Place your arms behind you with hands palm down on the floor. Straighten your arms and turn your hands inward so that your fingertips point towards your back. Keep your hands flat.
  • Inhale and press up using your arms and lift your hips up towards the ceiling. Do not allow your torso, the core of your body, to sag but instead maintain a straight line from your shoulders to the heels of your feet.
  • Exhale once you have lifted your core. Maintain a slow breathing technique.
  • Drop your head so that it hangs backwards while the rest of you is taut and straight.
  • Tighten and release your thigh and buttock muscles.
  • Hold this formation for about either 30 seconds or a minute. Perform this exercise anywhere from 3 minutes to as long as you'd like.

Single Leg Internal/External Rotation

Target Body Part: Abs,Butt/Hips,Legs - Thighs

Equipment Needed: No Equipment

  • Stand with your feet pointed straight ahead and hip-width apart.
  • Balance on your left leg and lift your right knee to your waist.
  • Maintaining this leg position, bring your right leg across your body so your right knee crosses your left leg.
  • Continuing the sequence, bring your right leg back across the body.
  • Allow your right hip to open bringing your right knee toward the right side of the room.
  • Hold each point for a few seconds then return to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the recommended amount.
